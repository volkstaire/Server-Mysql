This is a report application for SIPLah Tisera.

Route:
-/order for order list within 1 week until now and /order/csv for download purposes in csv format.
-/orderDetail for detailed order list within 1 week until now and /orderDetail/csv for download purposes in csv format.
-/orderDetail/:id for specific detailed order list based on ID Order and /orderDetail/:id/csv for download purposes in csv format.
-/orderTD/:year for order list from merchant TD based on Year and /orderTD/:year/csv for download purposes in csv format.
-/orderPID/:year for order list from merchant PID based on Year and /orderPID/:year/csv for download purposes in csv format.
-/orderAll/:year for order list from all merchant based on Year and /orderAll/:year/csv for download purposes in csv format.
